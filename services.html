About CyberNova Analytics

history

Our History

Founded in 2021 in Gaborone, Botswana, CyberNova Analytics emerged from the recognition that Southern Africa's digital transformation was outpacing its cyber defences. A small team of security engineers, data scientists, and policy experts came together with a single purpose: to build a truly sovereign cybersecurity capability that keeps data within SADC borders while defending against the most advanced threats. Since then, we have grown to serve government ministries, financial institutions, energy providers, and hundreds of mid‑sized enterprises across the region, all powered by our proprietary AI‑driven Sentinel platform.

target

Mission

Deliver sovereign, AI‑driven cybersecurity solutions that protect Southern African enterprises, governments, and critical infrastructure — ensuring data stays local, threats are neutralised in real time, and organisations can operate without fear of digital disruption.

visibility

Vision

To become the most trusted cybersecurity partner in the Southern African Development Community — a name synonymous with digital sovereignty, innovation, and uncompromising defence. By 2030, we aim to protect 5,000 regional organisations and train a new generation of African cyber professionals through our academy.
