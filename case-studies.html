Logistics · Durban, SA

Ransomware Incident Response

Ransomware

Challenge

LockBit ransomware encrypted 100% of operational systems at a Durban freight company, 72 hours before a critical port shipment window worth R12 million. All backups appeared compromised.

Solution

CyberNova's CIRT activated within 38 minutes. Offline tape backups were identified and validated. Network segmentation halted lateral spread. Forensic decryption recovered 23% of files independently.

Outcome

✓ Restored in 18 hours ✓ Zero ransom paid ✓ Shipment met

Phishing

Challenge
